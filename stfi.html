Long-term national lens

STFI – Somali Tourism Futures Initiative

A SISTRAD-designed flagship initiative framing tourism as a long-term economic, cultural, and environmental futures question – with scenario planning, investment pathways, and policy reform at its core.

View all flagship programs

Tourism Futures, Not Promotion

STFI focuses on strategic foresight and policy, not short-term marketing.

Why “Futures”?

Tourism in Somalia is often discussed in terms of promotion or recovery. STFI reframes the conversation around futures: alternative scenarios, risks, opportunities, and the policy and investment choices that will shape the sector over decades.

This long-term, national lens helps government, investors, and communities make better decisions today, rather than reacting to short-term pressures alone.

Scope of the Initiative

STFI explores scenario planning, investment and infrastructure pipelines, regulatory and policy reform options, and stakeholder dialogues. It is designed to produce evidence and frameworks that can guide national policy and private sector strategy.

The initiative is endorsed by relevant federal authorities and remains institutionally owned and stewarded by SISTRAD.

Core Workstreams

Structured streams of work under the STFI umbrella.

Scenario Planning & Strategic Foresight

Development of plausible long-term scenarios for Somali tourism – considering security, infrastructure, environment, and regional dynamics – to inform policy and investment dialogue.

Investment & Infrastructure Pipelines

Mapping of tourism-related investment needs and infrastructure priorities, and identification of pipeline opportunities that align with national development goals.

Policy & Regulatory Reform

Analysis and options for policy and regulatory reform that can enable sustainable, inclusive tourism development while managing social and environmental risks.

Stakeholder Dialogues & Learning

Convening of government, private sector, communities, and development partners around shared evidence and scenarios to build alignment and capture learning.

Ownership & Stewardship

STFI is a SISTRAD flagship initiative.

STFI is designed and stewarded by SISTRAD. Donors provide financing and support; government provides endorsement and policy alignment. Detailed program documentation and governance arrangements are shared with formal partners. As with all SISTRAD flagships, the institute retains intellectual and institutional ownership of the program design and coordinates research, analysis, and learning.
